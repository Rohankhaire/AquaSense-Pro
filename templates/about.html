How PureCast Predicts Water Quality

The Hybrid AI Engine

PureCast does not rely on simple if-then rules. It creates a robust prediction using a Hybrid Stacking Ensemble Model. By combining two powerful algorithms, we minimize error and maximize reliability across diverse water conditions.

1. Random Forest Regressor

Builds 250+ decision trees to analyze non-linear relationships between chemical parameters like pH and Dissolved Oxygen.

2. XGBoost (Extreme Gradient Boosting)

Uses gradient descent optimization to refine predictions and handle outliers in sensor data effectively.

8-Point Analysis Vector

Our model analyzes these critical chemical markers simultaneously:

  • pH & Conductivity (Ionic Balance)
  • Turbidity & TDS (Physical Suspensions)
  • Chlorine & Nitrate (Chemical Pollutants)
  • Dissolved Oxygen (Biological Health)

Satellite Remote Sensing

When local sensors aren't available, we query the Sentinel-2 Satellite constellation via Google Earth Engine.
